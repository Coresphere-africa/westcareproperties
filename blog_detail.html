Find Your Dream House in Nairobi

Finding your dream home in Nairobi can be an exciting but challenging process. At Westcare Properties Ltd, we understand that every client has unique needs and preferences. Whether you're looking for a modern apartment in Westlands, a family home in Karen, or investment property in emerging areas, our experienced agents are here to guide you through every step of the process.

Our comprehensive property search service includes detailed market analysis, property inspections, negotiation support, and legal guidance. We work with verified properties only, ensuring transparency and peace of mind for our clients. With over a decade of experience in the Kenyan real estate market, we've helped hundreds of families and investors find their perfect property.


Your dream home is more than just a building - it's where memories are made and futures are built. Let Westcare Properties help you find that perfect place.

Why Choose Westcare Properties?

When searching for property in Nairobi, choosing the right real estate partner can make all the difference. Westcare Properties stands out for our commitment to transparency, local market expertise, and personalized service. We don't just sell properties - we help you make informed decisions that align with your lifestyle and investment goals.
